What is coding?

Coding is a tool for mathematics.
Through learning fun coding, kids will learn how mathematics is closely related to coding.

It is also a tool that helps improve logical thinking skills, guiding kids to reach out to different areas successfully. Coding is demanded and required. At the same time, learning to code will advantage and benefit your kids in future.

What is STEM and how do we teach it?

STEM education stands for: Science Technology Engineering Mathematics. STEM is very important today and in futrue. We encourage kids to learn coding, robotics and multimedia software as they enjoy.

Scratch

Using Scratch Projects, kids improve:
  • Problem-solving (Debugging)
  • Logical and Critical thinking skills
  • Coding simulations of what happens in everyday life
naturally while having the interests to learn and enjoy our programs.
